About Word from the peak

Word from the peak was created to allow mountaineers, climbers and all of us who love exploring the more untouched parts of nature, to work together and make the knowledge we have available to others whilst also learning from each other. Word from the Peak is a place for people all over the world to learn (and teach) about the adventures that are just waiting to be had all over the world.
